About Oxia Palus

Oxia Palus is an AI art collective co-founded by Anthony Bourached in 2019. The collective gained international attention in September 2019 for reconstructing lost Picasso paintings using neural networks, reaching press headlines worldwide.

The project combines cutting-edge machine learning techniques with art history and conservation, using AI to recover and reconstruct artworks that have been lost, damaged, or painted over throughout history.
