Summer of High Performance Computing (SoHPC)

In 2015, I participated in SoHPC. Summer of HPC is a PRACE programme that offers summer placements at HPC centres across Europe to late stage undergraduates and early stage postgraduate students. Up to 20 top applicants from across Europe are selected to participate. Participants spend two months working on projects related to PRACE scientific or industrial work and ideally produce a visualisation or video of their results.

Participating in PRACE was a great experience. It began by a training week in Barcelona where we got a chance to meet all the other participants. Then I travelled with two fellow participants to start my project in Ostrava, Czech Republic. My blog posts during this time can be found in the links to the right, as well as my final presentation.
